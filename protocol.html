Brand Color Palette

This protocol defines the official color usage for consistency and visual identity.

Primary Colors

  • Royal Blue (#002366): Headers, banners, call-to-action sections
  • Gold (#FFD700): Buttons, icons, borders, logo highlights

Supporting Accents

  • Ivory/Cream (#FAF8F1): Soft background for elegance
  • Charcoal (#2E2E2E): Primary text color
  • Blush Pink (#F2D5D5): Optional creative accent

Creative Highlight

Use blush pink sparingly to introduce warmth and modern creativity.
